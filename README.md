# Global AI Job Market Trends & Salary Insights

## About This Project

I created this project as part of my Data Analytics learning journey to explore trends in the AI job market using data visualization.

Using a dataset of 15,000 AI-related job records, I analyzed how factors such as experience level, company size, remote work opportunities, industry type, and skills are connected to salary and career growth. The goal was not only to create charts but also to understand what the data says about the current AI job market.

This project focuses on transforming raw data into clear visual insights that can help students, job seekers, and professionals better understand the AI industry. By exploring different aspects of the dataset, I aimed to identify patterns that influence salaries, workplace flexibility, and career opportunities in AI-related roles.

## Dataset

* Records: 15,000
* Features: 19
* File: `ai_job_dataset.csv`

The dataset contains information about AI jobs, salaries, work arrangements, required skills, company details, benefits, and other job-related attributes. It provides a broad view of the AI job market and allows for the exploration of multiple factors that affect employment trends and compensation.

## Key Takeaways

* Salary generally increases with experience level.
* Some industries consistently appear among higher-paying AI jobs.
* Technical skills play an important role in accessing better opportunities.
* Benefits and flexibility can be just as important as salary when evaluating job attractiveness.
* Remote work has become a significant part of the AI job market.
* Experience level remains one of the strongest indicators of salary growth.
* Companies offering better benefits and flexibility often provide more attractive opportunities for professionals.
